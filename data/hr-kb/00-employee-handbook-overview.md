## 1. About Tulpix

Tulpix B.V. is a software company headquartered at Herengracht 482, 1017 CB Amsterdam, the Netherlands. We operate offices in Rotterdam, Berlin, Paris, Madrid, and Dublin. Tulpix was founded in 2014 and currently employs approximately 1,150 people across the European Union.

Our official statutory entity is **Tulpix B.V.** (KvK 67890123). EU subsidiaries operate under local entities (Tulpix GmbH, Tulpix SAS, Tulpix Iberia S.L., Tulpix Ireland Ltd.).

## 2. Purpose of this handbook

This handbook is the authoritative source of HR policies for Tulpix employees. It complements — but does not replace — your individual employment contract, the applicable Collective Labour Agreement (CAO) where relevant, Dutch labor law (Burgerlijk Wetboek Boek 7), and EU directives.

Where local law in a subsidiary country provides more favorable terms than this handbook, **local law prevails**.

## 4. How updates work

Policies are reviewed annually by the People & Culture team. Material changes are communicated via:

1. An all-hands email at least 30 days before the effective date.
2. A summary in the monthly People newsletter.
3. A pinned post in the `#people-announcements` Slack channel.

Minor editorial updates (typo fixes, contact changes) may be made without notice.

## 6. Languages

The official version of this handbook is English. Dutch, German, French, and Spanish translations are available on the intranet under *People > Handbook*. In case of discrepancy, the **English version prevails** — except where Dutch law requires the Dutch text to be authoritative (e.g., the employment contract itself).
