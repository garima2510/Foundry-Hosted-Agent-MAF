# Training & Development Policy

**Document ID:** HR-700
**Version:** 2.2
**Effective date:** 1 January 2026
**Owner:** Talent & Development (learning@tulpix.example)
**Applies to:** All Tulpix employees (with pro-ration for part-time)

---

## 1. Our learning philosophy

Tulpix follows the **70-20-10 model**:

- **70%** of growth comes from **on-the-job experience** (stretch assignments, projects, rotations).
- **20%** from **learning from others** (coaching, mentoring, sponsorship, peer learning).
- **10%** from **structured learning** (courses, conferences, certifications).

We invest in all three.

## 2. Learning budget

Every full-time employee has an **annual personal learning budget of €2,000**, pro-rated for part-time. The budget is per calendar year and does not roll over.

### 2.1 What it can be used for

- Online courses and platforms (Coursera, O'Reilly, Pluralsight, LinkedIn Learning, etc.)
- Books, e-books, audiobooks
- Conferences and workshops (in-person and virtual)
- Certifications and exam fees (e.g., AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, CKA, PMP, ScrumMaster)
- Coaching (executive, career, leadership)
- Language classes (priority: language of your office country)
- University courses (audit or credit)
- Professional memberships

### 2.2 What requires manager approval

- Any single item over €1,000.
- Multi-day conferences (which often combine travel and event costs).
- Certifications with significant exam fees (>€500).

### 2.3 What is not eligible

- Coursework unrelated to your role or career direction (e.g., personal hobbies). Use your wellbeing budget instead.
- Equipment (use the home office allowance).
- Subscriptions you would have paid for personally (e.g., Netflix, Spotify).

### 2.4 Submission

Submit via Concur using the **Learning** category. Pre-purchase whenever possible — Tulpix can be billed directly for many platforms.

## 3. Learning time

You may use **up to 5% of working time per quarter (≈ 4 days)** for self-directed learning during work hours, with manager awareness. Block "learning time" on your calendar.

For long-form study (e.g., evening university courses, certifications):

- Time off for exams: paid, up to 5 days per year.
- Study leave for major qualifications: case-by-case, may be partially paid.

## 4. Skill paths

### 4.1 Engineering

- **Tulpix Tech Path** — internal curriculum, mapping skills against levels (L1 to L8).
- Open-source contribution time: 1 day/quarter to contribute to OSS used by Tulpix.
- Internal "Lunch & Learn" series, weekly.

### 4.2 Product, Design

- **Product fundamentals** (for new PMs) — 6-week cohort program.
- **Discovery techniques** workshops — quarterly.
- Design critiques and portfolio reviews — bi-weekly.

### 4.3 Sales, CSM

- **Tulpix Sales Academy** — at hire (3 weeks) plus quarterly refreshers.
- MEDDPICC, command of the message, value-selling — recurring training.

### 4.4 Operations, Finance, People

- Function-specific external programs (CIPD, ACCA, Bekkers Procurement, etc.) — annual nominations.

### 4.5 Leadership

- **Tulpix Manager Foundations** — required for all new managers within 90 days.
- **Tulpix Lead** — 9-month leadership development program for M4+.
- **Executive coaching** — 6 sessions/year for M5+ managers.

## 5. Mentoring and sponsorship

- **Mentoring** — open to everyone via the internal *MentorMatch* platform. Time commitment: ~1 hour/month per pairing.
- **Sponsorship** — for under-represented groups, see `11-dei-policy.md`. Sponsors are senior leaders who actively advocate for protégés.
- **Reverse mentoring** — junior employees pair with senior leaders to share fresh perspectives.

## 6. Performance and growth

### 6.1 Feedback cadence

- **1:1s** — weekly, owned by the employee.
- **Quarterly check-ins** — written, lightweight.
- **Annual performance review** — full review, two-way, with calibration.

### 6.2 Performance ratings

Ratings used in calibration (employees see their own):

- **Exceeds Expectations** (~15%)
- **Strong Performance** (~70%)
- **Building Toward Expectations** (~10%)
- **Below Expectations** (~5%)

Ratings inform compensation and bonus decisions, but they are not the only input.

### 6.3 Promotion process

- Self-nomination is encouraged.
- Manager prepares a promotion package: scope, examples, peer feedback, alignment to next-level expectations.
- Calibration committee reviews.
- Decisions communicated within 4 weeks.
- Promotions effective at the start of the next month, with a bump in salary as per `07-compensation-and-salary.md` section 4.3.

## 7. Career growth conversations

A formal **career growth conversation** between employee and manager happens **at least twice a year** (May and November). It covers:

- Current strengths and growth areas
- Aspirations (next 1–3 years)
- Skills to develop
- Experiences to seek
- Support needed

The conversation is documented by the employee in Workday under *Growth*.

## 8. Internal mobility

Tulpix encourages employees to move between teams or functions every 2–4 years.

- **Internal-first principle:** Open roles are posted internally **5 days before** external posting.
- **Conversation first:** Talk to your manager early. Tulpix supports moves; managers do not block them unreasonably.
- **Transition period:** Default 1 month between announcement and start in the new role.
- **Salary on move:** Match the band for the new role. No salary cuts for lateral moves; exceptions for major function changes are agreed in writing.

## 9. International transfers

Tulpix offers short-term (≤ 3 months) and long-term (≥ 6 months) international assignments. Conditions:

- Sponsored by both the sending and receiving manager.
- Tax, immigration, and social security advisory provided by Bridgehead Tax B.V.
- Relocation support per the international assignment package (intranet).
- Return rights to the original team or an equivalent role.

## 10. Apprenticeships and graduate programs

- **Tulpix Grad** — 18-month rotational program for early-career hires.
- **Tulpix Apprentice** — 2-year apprenticeship for non-traditional entrants (mid-career changers, returners, refugees) in partnership with HackYourFuture and Refugee Talent Hub.

## 11. Recordkeeping

All learning activities funded by Tulpix are recorded in Workday under *Learning*. Certifications expire as per their issuing body; renewals are budgeted from the annual learning budget.

## 12. Departing employees

- The learning budget closes when employment ends.
- Certifications you earned remain yours.
- Tulpix may, at its discretion, ask for repayment of training costs over **€3,000 per item** if you leave within 12 months of completing the training. Pro-rated and capped at the actual training cost. This must be agreed in writing **before** the training starts (studie­kostenbeding under Dutch law).
