# Health, Safety & Well-being (Arbo) Policy

**Document ID:** HR-810
**Version:** 4.1
**Effective date:** 1 January 2026
**Owner:** Health & Safety Officer (arbo@tulpix.example)
**Applies to:** All Tulpix employees, interns, contractors on Tulpix premises

---

## 1. Purpose

Tulpix complies with the Dutch Working Conditions Act (**Arbeidsomstandighedenwet, "Arbowet"**) and the EU Framework Directive on Safety and Health at Work (Directive 89/391/EEC). This policy describes how we keep employees safe and healthy — physically and mentally — across all our offices and remote workplaces.

## 2. Roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Employer (Tulpix) | Provide a safe workplace, conduct RI&E, contract a certified Arbo service |
| H&S Officer (Preventiemedewerker) | First point of contact, runs RI&E, coordinates incidents |
| Company doctor (Bedrijfsarts) | Independent medical advice on sickness, return-to-work, occupational health |
| Works Council (OR) | Right of consent on H&S policy changes (WOR art. 27) |
| Managers | Implement controls, model safe behavior, listen to concerns |
| Employees | Use equipment safely, report hazards, follow instructions |

The certified Arbo service is **ArboNed** (contract until 2027). The company doctor is reachable via 088-002-1234 or bedrijfsarts@arboned.example.

## 3. Risk inventory and evaluation (RI&E)

We conduct a full **Risico-Inventarisatie & -Evaluatie** every 4 years and after any significant change (new office, hybrid policy change, major reorg). The current RI&E was completed in **March 2024** and is available on the intranet (`/people/arbo/rie-2024.pdf`).

The associated **Plan van Aanpak** is reviewed quarterly by the Works Council.

## 4. Office safety

### 4.1 General

- Fire wardens (BHV'ers) are trained per office, minimum **1 BHV'er per 50 occupants**.
- Fire drill: twice per year, unannounced.
- AED on every floor; AED training offered annually to all employees.
- First-aid kits at every kitchen and reception.
- Emergency contacts posted at every exit.

### 4.2 Workstation ergonomics

- Adjustable desk (sit/stand) and ergonomic chair are standard.
- Free workstation assessment by the Arbo service available on request.
- If the assessment recommends additional equipment (footrest, monitor arm, vertical mouse), Tulpix pays.

### 4.3 Indoor air, light, and noise

- CO₂ sensors in all meeting rooms; ventilation kicks in above 800 ppm.
- Daylight access for >80% of desks; circadian-friendly lighting after 17:00.
- Noise level target: <55 dB(A) at desks; quiet rooms available for focused work.

## 5. Remote workplace safety

For employees working from home (see HR-310):

- A **home-office self-assessment** must be completed annually in the HRIS.
- Tulpix provides a one-time €750 home-office setup allowance plus a €40/month remote allowance (Dutch tax-free thuiswerkvergoeding).
- The Arbo service offers a virtual ergonomic consultation on request.
- Tulpix is **not liable** for home accidents outside the agreed work setup, but workplace injuries during work hours are covered.

## 7. Mental health and well-being

### 7.1 Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

Provided by **OpenUp**. Free, confidential, available 24/7 in EN/NL/DE/FR/ES.

- Up to **8 sessions per year** with a psychologist.
- Self-guided programs (sleep, stress, burnout prevention).
- Crisis line: 0800-0113.

Managers cannot see whether an employee uses the EAP.

### 7.2 Burnout prevention

- Quarterly pulse survey includes a validated burnout indicator (UBOS-K, 6 questions).
- High-risk teams receive a follow-up workshop.
- Managers must complete a **2-hour mental-health-first-aid training** within 90 days of becoming a people manager.
- Right to disconnect (see HR-300): no expectation of response outside working hours.

### 7.3 Bereavement, life events

- 5 working days bereavement leave for partner/child/parent (paid).
- 2 working days for sibling, grandparent, parent-in-law (paid).
- Additional unpaid leave by agreement.
- Counsellor referral via the EAP.

## 8. Pregnancy and lactation

Per the Working Conditions Act and the Arbobesluit:

- Pregnancy must not affect job security, role, or pay.
- A risk assessment is mandatory within **2 weeks** of notification.
- Reasonable adjustments: flexible hours, no night shifts, no exposure to known risks.
- A private **lactation room** (not a toilet) is available at every Tulpix office.
- Paid lactation breaks up to **25% of working time** for the first **9 months** after birth (Arbeidstijdenwet art. 4:8).

## 9. Workplace violence, harassment, and bullying (PSA)

Tulpix has zero tolerance for **psychosociale arbeidsbelasting (PSA)**: aggression, sexual harassment, bullying, discrimination, and work-related stress.

- Two **vertrouwenspersonen** (confidential counsellors), one internal and one external (independent).
  - Internal: vertrouwenspersoon@tulpix.example
  - External: ArboNed confidential counsellor (088-002-1235)
- Reporting channels: see HR-520 grievance & whistleblower.
- Investigations follow the procedure in HR-520, with mandatory PSA-trained investigator.

## 10. Substance use

- Alcohol: a glass of wine/beer at company events is acceptable; intoxication on the job is not.
- Drugs: illegal drug use during work hours is grounds for dismissal.
- Anyone struggling with addiction can confidentially contact the company doctor or EAP without disciplinary consequences for seeking help.

## 11. Accidents and incidents

Report **all** accidents — even minor — within **24 hours**:

1. To your manager.
2. To the H&S Officer via arbo@tulpix.example.
3. Serious accidents (hospital admission, permanent injury, fatality) are reported to the **Inspectie SZW** within **24 hours** as required by Arbowet art. 9.

The H&S Officer maintains an incident register, reviewed quarterly with the Works Council.

## 12. Working with display screens (Arbobesluit hoofdstuk 5)

- Eye exam: every 2 years, paid by Tulpix.
- Computer glasses (special prescription for screen distance): up to **€150 every 3 years**, prescription-based.
- Mandatory micro-break reminders enabled in the workstation software (can be disabled by request).

## 13. Travel safety

- See HR-600 for travel policy. H&S add-ons:
  - Pre-travel briefing for high-risk destinations (provided by International SOS).
  - Vaccinations paid by Tulpix where required by the destination.
  - Travel insurance covers medical, repatriation, and accident.

## 14. Smoking

- All Tulpix offices and company cars are **smoke-free** (including e-cigarettes), per Tabaks- en rookwarenwet.
- Designated outdoor smoking areas at offices that have outdoor space.
- Smoking-cessation program available via the EAP.
