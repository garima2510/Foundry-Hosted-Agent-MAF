# Travel & Expense Policy

**Document ID:** HR-600
**Version:** 4.5
**Effective date:** 1 January 2026
**Owner:** Finance — AP team (expenses@tulpix.example)
**Applies to:** All Tulpix employees and approved contractors travelling on Tulpix business

---

## 1. Principles

- **Spend like it's your own money.** Choose value, not luxury.
- **Travel only when it adds clear value.** Consider video conferencing as the default.
- **Be fair, transparent, and timely** in all reporting.
- **Travel sustainably:** prefer rail, electric vehicles, and direct routes; offset flights through the corporate booking tool.
- **Comply with safety and security rules**, including the Tulpix Travel Risk Policy.

## 2. Booking

### 2.1 Booking tool

All travel must be booked through **TripActions / Navan** (https://navan.tulpix.example) using the corporate Tulpix profile. This ensures:

- Duty-of-care location tracking
- Cost visibility and policy enforcement
- Carbon offset
- Centralized invoicing

Personal credit cards may be used only when Navan is unavailable; reimburse via Concur (see section 6).

### 2.2 Advance booking

- Domestic / intra-EU flights and trains: at least **14 days** in advance where possible.
- Hotels for events: book within **7 days** of confirmation.
- Last-minute bookings (<7 days) require manager approval and a brief justification in Navan.

## 3. Transport

### 3.1 Rail (preferred)

For journeys under **6 hours door-to-door** within the EU, **rail is mandatory** unless rail cost is more than 2× the air fare or itineraries do not allow it. This applies to routes such as Amsterdam–Paris, Amsterdam–Berlin, Amsterdam–London (Eurostar), Paris–Frankfurt, etc.

- **Class:** Standard / 1st class for journeys >2 hours; standard otherwise.
- **NS-Business Card:** for Tulpix NL employees, used for all domestic Dutch rail.

### 3.2 Air

When flying:

- **Economy** for all flights up to **6 hours**.
- **Premium economy** allowed for flights over 6 hours.
- **Business class** allowed only for:
  - Flights over **8 hours** with same-day work commitments on arrival.
  - Health-related needs documented by the company doctor.
  - Senior leaders (M5+) at their discretion.
- One **checked bag** is reimbursable for trips of 3+ nights.

### 3.3 Car

- **Rental cars:** compact or mid-size category. Premium and SUV require justification.
- **Electric vehicles preferred** where the rental supplier offers them at comparable price.
- **Personal car for business:** reimbursed at €0.23/km tax-free.
- **Taxis / Uber:** allowed when public transport is impractical (late hours, with luggage, in unsafe areas). Receipt required for any single trip over €25.

### 3.4 Public transport at destination

Public transport is the default for short trips at the destination. Multi-day passes preferred.

## 4. Lodging

| City tier | Per night cap (excl. VAT and city tax) |
|-----------|----------------------------------------|
| Tier 1 (Amsterdam, Paris, London, Dublin, Stockholm, Zurich, Geneva) | €260 |
| Tier 2 (Berlin, Madrid, Barcelona, Frankfurt, Munich, Brussels) | €210 |
| Tier 3 (other EU cities) | €170 |
| Tier 4 (smaller cities, towns) | €140 |

- Caps may be exceeded by up to 20% during conferences in the venue's recommended hotels (with manager approval).
- Single-occupancy rooms are the default. Suites and executive floors are not reimbursable.
- Mini-bar and in-room movies are not reimbursable.

## 5. Meals

### 5.1 Per diem (NL — fiscally compliant)

Tulpix uses the **CAO Rijk** per diem table for NL employees, fitting within Belastingdienst zakelijke representatie norms:

| Meal | Per diem (NL trip) | Per diem (international) |
|------|--------------------|-----------------------|
| Breakfast | €13.50 | €17.00 |
| Lunch | €17.00 | €22.00 |
| Dinner | €28.00 | €40.00 |

Per diems do not require receipts but must be claimed only for meals **actually taken** (i.e., not when the employer or host provides a meal).

### 5.2 Expense-based (other countries)

In Germany, France, Spain, and Ireland, claim actual costs against the local equivalent caps. See country addenda on the intranet.

### 5.3 Hospitality

When hosting customers or partners:

- Up to **€85 per attendee** for meals.
- Higher amounts require pre-approval from the line manager and the budget owner.
- Always include the names and affiliations of attendees in the expense description.
- Alcohol may be included in moderation.

## 6. Submission and reimbursement

### 6.1 Tools

- **Concur Expense** (https://concur.tulpix.example) for all expenses.
- Snap photos of receipts via the Concur mobile app.
- All currencies auto-converted at the daily ECB exchange rate at the date of expense.

### 6.2 Deadlines

- Submit expense reports **within 30 days** of the trip end or expense date.
- Late submissions (>60 days) require CFO approval and may be denied.
- Manager approval target: **5 business days**.
- Reimbursement: paid with the next monthly payroll cycle after approval (cut-off the 15th of each month).

### 6.3 Receipts

- Receipts required for **any expense €25 or more**.
- Restaurant receipts must show items (a credit card slip alone is insufficient).
- Lost receipts: a missing-receipt declaration in Concur is allowed up to **3 times per year per employee**.

## 7. What is not reimbursable

- Personal entertainment (cinema, sports events) unless explicitly part of a customer/partner activity.
- Spa, gym, mini-bar.
- Speeding, parking, or other traffic fines.
- Travel insurance (Tulpix's corporate insurance covers business travel).
- Loyalty program upgrades or fees (e.g., paying for a fast-track lane on personal preference).
- Non-business trip extensions (you may extend a trip personally — book separately and pay personally).
- Family or partner travel (unless on a Tulpix-approved relocation or international assignment).

## 8. Corporate cards

- Issued to employees with frequent travel or supplier interactions, on manager request.
- The card is for **business expenses only**.
- Misuse (personal expenses, cash advances without approval) is a Code of Conduct breach.
- Expenses on the card must be reconciled in Concur within 30 days.

## 9. Expense controls

- Random audits by the Finance team on 5% of all expense reports each month.
- Targeted audits triggered by anomalies (round numbers, weekend hospitality, repeated lost receipts).
- Findings of misconduct are referred to HR per `12-grievance-and-whistleblower.md`.

## 10. Travel safety and well-being

- **Pre-trip check:** Navan flags advisories from International SOS for the destination.
- **Crisis line:** International SOS +44 20 8762 8133 (24/7, Tulpix membership 27AYCA00xxxx).
- **Health:** Vaccinations, malaria prophylaxis, and travel kits reimbursed for high-risk destinations.
- **Crossing time zones:** for trips with >5 hour time difference and same-day meetings on arrival, lodging may be booked the night before.
- **Rest after long flights:** if you fly more than 6 hours overnight on Sunday, you may take Monday off (or work from home).

## 11. Sustainability

- Tulpix offsets 100% of business flights via Atmosfair / Climeworks.
- Annual flight budget per employee: **8 round-trip equivalents** within EU. Beyond that requires director-level approval.
- Train + ferry combinations are encouraged for cross-EU travel.
- Office-to-airport transfers: prefer public transport or rideshare (UberPool / BlaBlaCar) where safe.
