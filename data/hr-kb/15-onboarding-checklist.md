# Onboarding Checklist & New Joiner Guide

**Document ID:** HR-710
**Version:** 3.1
**Effective date:** 1 January 2026
**Owner:** People Operations (onboarding@tulpix.example)
**Applies to:** All new hires across Tulpix B.V. and EU subsidiaries

---

## 1. Purpose

This document describes the end-to-end onboarding journey at Tulpix, from offer acceptance to the end of the probation period. The goal is for every new joiner to feel **welcomed, equipped, and productive within 90 days**.

Onboarding is a shared responsibility:

- **People Operations** owns the process and tooling.
- **The hiring manager** owns the new joiner's success.
- **The buddy** owns day-to-day questions and social integration.
- **The new joiner** owns their learning and asks for help when needed.

## 2. Probation period

| Country | Standard probation | Maximum allowed by law |
|---------|--------------------|------------------------|
| Netherlands | 1 month (contract <2 yrs), 2 months (≥2 yrs or indefinite) | 2 months |
| Germany | 6 months | 6 months |
| France | 4 months (cadres), renewable to 8 | 4–8 months by category |
| Spain | 6 months (qualified roles) | 6 months |
| Ireland | 6 months, extendable to 11 | 12 months |

During probation, either party may terminate with the legally required notice (often shorter than post-probation). Probation outcomes are decided in writing at least **2 weeks before the end** of the period.

## 3. Pre-boarding (between offer signed and Day 1)

Owned by **People Operations**.

- [ ] Send signed contract and welcome pack within 2 business days of countersignature.
- [ ] Collect statutory documents:
  - NL: copy of valid ID (passport or EU/EEA ID card — driver's license is **not** acceptable under the Wet op de identificatieplicht), BSN, IBAN, payroll tax statement (Loonheffingenformulier), proof of address.
  - DE: Personalausweis or passport, Steuer-ID, Sozialversicherungsnummer, IBAN, health insurance details.
  - FR: pièce d'identité, numéro de sécurité sociale, RIB, carte vitale.
  - IE: PPS number, passport/ID, IBAN.
  - ES: DNI/NIE, número de afiliación a la Seguridad Social, IBAN.
- [ ] Run right-to-work check (mandatory for non-EU/EEA nationals; document retained for 5 years after end of employment per Vreemdelingenwet).
- [ ] Initiate 30% ruling application if eligible (see HR-420).
- [ ] Order laptop, peripherals, and access badge (delivered ≥3 business days before Day 1).
- [ ] Create accounts: Microsoft Entra ID, Slack, GitHub, HRIS (Bob), expense tool (Pleo), VPN.
- [ ] Send Day 1 logistics email: office address, arrival time (10:00), what to bring, dress code (smart casual).
- [ ] Hiring manager assigns a **buddy** (not in reporting line, ideally same office).
- [ ] Hiring manager drafts a 30/60/90 day plan in the HRIS.

## 4. Day 1

Owned by **People Operations + hiring manager + buddy**.

| Time | Activity | Owner |
|------|----------|-------|
| 10:00 | Welcome at reception, badge collection, office tour | Office Manager |
| 10:30 | Laptop handover and first login | IT |
| 11:00 | HR welcome session: handbook, payroll, benefits enrollment | People Ops |
| 12:30 | Team lunch (paid by company) | Hiring manager |
| 14:00 | 1:1 with hiring manager: role expectations, 30/60/90 plan | Hiring manager |
| 15:00 | Buddy walkthrough: tools, channels, where to find what | Buddy |
| 16:30 | Optional: meet the team (informal coffee) | Team |

If Day 1 falls on a public holiday, onboarding starts the next business day.

## 5. Week 1 — "Settle in"

- [ ] Complete mandatory e-learning (deadline: end of Week 1):
  - Code of Conduct (HR-500)
  - GDPR & Data Protection (HR-800) — required by Art. 39 GDPR
  - Information Security Awareness
  - Health & Safety basics (HR-810)
  - Anti-harassment and bullying
- [ ] Enroll in pension scheme (auto-enrollment, opt-out within 30 days — see HR-410).
- [ ] Choose benefits (commuter allowance, bike scheme, meal vouchers where applicable).
- [ ] Set up Multi-Factor Authentication and password manager (1Password).
- [ ] Read your top 3 most-relevant policy documents with your buddy.
- [ ] Attend Tulpix 101 session (Tuesdays at 14:00, virtual).
- [ ] Submit a "first impressions" survey (anonymous, 5 questions).

## 6. Month 1 — "Learn and contribute"

- [ ] Shadow at least 3 colleagues across functions.
- [ ] Ship a small, scoped piece of work ("first commit / first deck / first ticket resolved").
- [ ] 30-day check-in with hiring manager (structured template in HRIS).
- [ ] 30-day check-in with People Ops (15 min).
- [ ] Complete the role-specific learning path (engineering, sales, support, etc.).

## 7. Month 2 — "Build momentum"

- [ ] Take ownership of a recurring responsibility.
- [ ] Present at a team meeting ("what I've learned so far").
- [ ] 60-day manager check-in.
- [ ] Confirm benefits enrollment is complete.
- [ ] Plan first vacation days (we encourage you to book time off — it's not a sign of being uncommitted).

## 8. Month 3 — "Probation review"

- [ ] Probation conversation with manager **at least 2 weeks before** probation ends.
- [ ] Written confirmation of successful probation (or, if not, formal next steps).
- [ ] 90-day People Ops survey ("Onboarding NPS").
- [ ] Buddy relationship formally ends (but most stay informal).

## 9. The buddy program

A buddy is a peer assigned to support the new joiner socially and tactically. Buddies are **not** mentors or managers.

### 9.1 Buddy responsibilities

- Coffee or lunch in Week 1, then weekly check-ins for 90 days.
- Answer "stupid questions" without judgment.
- Help navigate informal norms (Slack etiquette, meeting culture, who to ask for what).
- Flag any onboarding issues to the hiring manager or People Ops.

### 9.2 Becoming a buddy

Volunteers sign up via the HRIS. Buddies receive a 1-hour training and a €50 voucher per buddy completed.

## 11. Onboarding for specific cohorts

### 11.1 Interns and working students

- Shorter onboarding (1 week formal, 1 buddy, no probation under Dutch law for fixed-term <6 months).
- Mandatory: Code of Conduct, GDPR, H&S basics. Other e-learning is optional.

### 11.2 Returning employees ("boomerangs")

- Light-touch onboarding (½ day instead of full Day 1).
- HRIS, payroll, and benefits restart from scratch — including pension auto-enrollment.

### 11.3 Internal transfers

- New manager owns a 30/60/90 plan even though the employee is not new to Tulpix.
- No re-enrollment in benefits.

### 11.4 International relocations

- People Ops Mobility team supports visa, housing search (up to 30 days temporary accommodation paid), school search for dependants, and tax briefing.
- Relocation allowance: €5,000 (single), €8,000 (with partner), €10,000 (with partner + children). Taxable unless covered by 30% ruling.

## 12. If onboarding goes wrong

If you feel unsupported, lost, or that your role is not what was described:

1. Raise it with your hiring manager first.
2. If unresolved, contact People Ops (onboarding@tulpix.example).
3. If still unresolved, use the grievance procedure (HR-520).

We treat onboarding feedback as a leading indicator of retention. Speak up early.
