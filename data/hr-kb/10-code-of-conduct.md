# Code of Conduct

**Document ID:** HR-500
**Version:** 5.1
**Effective date:** 1 January 2026
**Owner:** People & Culture, Legal, Ethics & Compliance
**Applies to:** All Tulpix employees, contractors, interns, and Board members

---

## 1. Our promise

At Tulpix we build software that thousands of businesses depend on. The way we behave matters as much as what we build. This Code of Conduct describes the standards every Tulpix person commits to.

If you are unsure whether something is allowed, ask. The most important question is not "Is this against the rules?" but "Would I be comfortable explaining this to a colleague, a customer, or a journalist?"

## 2. Respect for people

### 2.1 Inclusive workplace

We treat each other with **respect, fairness, and curiosity**. Discrimination, harassment, bullying, intimidation, and retaliation are prohibited — at the office, on Slack, in customer interactions, at conferences, in informal events, and online.

Protected characteristics under EU and Dutch law include (non-exhaustive): race, ethnicity, nationality, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, religion, belief, disability, chronic illness, marital status, pregnancy, parental status, political opinion, and trade union membership.

### 2.2 Sexual harassment

Sexual harassment is any unwanted conduct of a sexual nature that violates dignity or creates an intimidating, hostile, degrading, humiliating, or offensive environment. This includes: unwanted physical contact, sexual comments or jokes, sharing sexual imagery, persistent unwanted attention, and quid pro quo proposals.

Tulpix has a **zero-tolerance policy**. Reports are handled confidentially and seriously through the procedures in `12-grievance-and-whistleblower.md`.

### 2.3 Bystander expectation

If you witness conduct that does not align with this Code, **say something** — directly to the person, to a manager, to HR, or via the confidential channels.

## 3. Honesty and integrity

### 3.1 Truthful communication

- Be accurate and complete in everything you write or sign — code, documents, contracts, expense claims, performance reviews, and customer commitments.
- Never knowingly mislead a customer, a colleague, an investor, an auditor, or a regulator.

### 3.2 Conflicts of interest

A conflict of interest exists when your personal interests could influence — or appear to influence — your judgment at work. Examples:

- Holding a financial interest in a competitor, supplier, or customer.
- Hiring or managing a close family member.
- Side jobs (nevenwerkzaamheden) that overlap with Tulpix business.

You must **disclose actual or potential conflicts** to your manager and the Ethics & Compliance team via ethics@tulpix.example. Disclosure does not automatically prevent the activity — many conflicts can be managed through transparency or recusal.

### 3.3 Side activities

Under Dutch law (post-2022 Wet implementatie EU-richtlijn transparante en voorspelbare arbeidsvoorwaarden), Tulpix may only restrict your ability to take a second job if there is an **objective justification** (e.g., conflict of interest, working time limits, confidentiality, health and safety). Disclose all side activities via Workday for assessment.

### 3.4 Gifts and hospitality

Modest, infrequent business hospitality is acceptable. Stricter rules apply when dealing with public officials.

| Category | Limit per recipient per year | Notes |
|----------|------------------------------|-------|
| Gifts received | €75 | Items above this must be declined or shared with the team |
| Meals or events received | €150 per occasion | Must have a clear business purpose |
| Gifts given to private sector | €100 | Manager approval above this |
| Gifts to public officials | €25 | Often prohibited entirely; check with Legal |
| Cash or cash equivalents | Not allowed | Includes gift cards |

Log all received gifts above €25 in Workday under *Compliance > Gifts*.

## 4. Protecting Tulpix and customer information

### 4.1 Confidential information

Treat Tulpix and customer information as confidential by default. Do not share confidential information outside Tulpix unless authorized. Do not discuss confidential matters in public places, on personal social media, or with friends and family.

### 4.2 Customer data

Customer data is governed by Tulpix's Data Protection Policy, the GDPR, and customer Data Processing Agreements. Access only the data you need, only for the purpose authorized, and only for as long as necessary. See `16-data-protection-gdpr.md`.

### 4.3 Insider information

Tulpix is privately held; however, we hold material non-public information about customers, partners, and prospective acquisitions. Trading on such information may breach the EU Market Abuse Regulation (596/2014). When in doubt, do not trade.

### 4.4 Devices and accounts

- Use Tulpix-issued devices for work where possible.
- Do not install unauthorized software on Tulpix devices.
- Do not use personal email or unauthorized cloud storage for work data.
- Report lost or stolen devices to security@tulpix.example within **2 hours**.

## 5. Use of AI tools

### 5.1 Approved tools

Tulpix permits the use of approved AI tools (e.g., GitHub Copilot, Microsoft Copilot, internal Tulpix Assistant). The current list is on the intranet under *IT > Approved Software*.

### 5.2 What you may not do

- Submit Tulpix or customer confidential information into a non-approved third-party AI service (free ChatGPT, free Claude, etc.).
- Generate content that is misleading, defamatory, or infringes IP.
- Use AI-generated code without review and testing.
- Bypass security or licensing controls (e.g., use of AI to extract or reproduce copyrighted source code).

### 5.3 EU AI Act

We comply with the EU AI Act (2024/1689), including transparency obligations for AI-generated content shared with customers. Tag AI-assisted content where required.

## 6. Workplace safety and substances

- Tulpix workplaces are free of harassment, intimidation, and violence.
- Possession or use of illegal drugs in the workplace, in company vehicles, or at company events is prohibited.
- Alcohol may be served at company events; **drink moderately** and never if you are returning to work duties or operating vehicles.
- We respect colleagues who do not drink — do not pressure them.

## 7. Social media and external communication

- Personal social media accounts are yours. If you mention Tulpix, make clear that views are your own.
- Do not share customer names, internal screenshots, or financial details.
- Only the Communications team may speak publicly on behalf of Tulpix. Press inquiries go to press@tulpix.example.
- If contacted by analysts, regulators, or law enforcement, route to legal@tulpix.example before responding.

## 8. Anti-bribery and corruption

Tulpix complies with the Dutch Wetboek van Strafrecht (anti-corruption articles), the UK Bribery Act, the US FCPA, and OECD Anti-Bribery Convention.

You must never:

- Offer, give, request, or accept anything of value with the intent to improperly influence a decision.
- Use intermediaries to circumvent these rules.
- Make facilitation payments (small "grease" payments to public officials).

When in doubt, ask ethics@tulpix.example.

## 9. Sanctions and trade

Tulpix complies with EU sanctions (CFSP Council Regulations), US OFAC, and UK OFSI sanctions. Do not provide products, services, or support to sanctioned parties or jurisdictions without legal review. The current restricted list is at `https://intranet.tulpix.example/legal/sanctions`.

## 10. Reporting concerns and non-retaliation

Speak up:

- **Confidential**: confidential@tulpix.example (CPO only).
- **Anonymous**: speak-up@tulpix.example or +31 20 555 0199 (24/7, run by independent provider Whistleblower Direct).
- **Manager**, **HR**, **Legal**, **Ethics**.
- **Works Council** (Ondernemingsraad), or@tulpix.example.

Tulpix prohibits retaliation against anyone who, in good faith, reports a concern, raises a question, or participates in an investigation. This is consistent with the Dutch Wet bescherming klokkenluiders and EU Whistleblowing Directive (2019/1937). See `12-grievance-and-whistleblower.md`.

## 11. Consequences

Violations of this Code may result in disciplinary action up to and including:

- Verbal or written warning
- Performance improvement plan
- Suspension
- Dismissal (with or without notice, depending on severity)
- Civil or criminal proceedings where laws have been broken

Decisions follow the principle of **proportionality** and the Dutch principles of due process under labor law.

## 12. Acknowledgement

All employees acknowledge this Code of Conduct on hire and at every annual update. Acknowledgement is recorded in Workday and is required to remain a Tulpix employee.
