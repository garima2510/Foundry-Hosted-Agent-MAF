# HR FAQ — Frequently Asked Questions

**Document ID:** HR-999
**Version:** 6.0
**Effective date:** 1 February 2026
**Owner:** People & Culture (people@tulpix.example)

This FAQ summarizes the most common questions Tulpix employees ask. For the full rules, see the linked policy. **In case of conflict, the policy document and applicable law prevail.**

---

## Leave & vacation

**Q: How many vacation days do I get?**
A: 25 statutory days + 5 Tulpix bonus days = **30 paid vacation days per year** for full-time employees in the Netherlands. Pro-rated for part-time and mid-year joiners. See HR-100.

**Q: When does the holiday allowance (vakantiegeld) get paid?**
A: 8% of your annual gross salary, paid in **May** by default. You may opt for monthly payout in the HRIS. See HR-400.

**Q: How long do my statutory days stay valid?**
A: **Statutory** days (20) expire **6 months** after the end of the year they were earned (so 2025 days expire 1 July 2026). **Bonus** days (5 Tulpix + 5 above-statutory) carry over for **5 years**. See HR-100.

**Q: Can I sell my vacation days back?**
A: Only the 5 Tulpix bonus days can be sold back, at base salary value, once per year (December cycle). Statutory days cannot be sold. See HR-100.

**Q: Can I buy extra vacation days?**
A: Yes — up to **5 extra days per year**, deducted from gross salary via WKR. Apply in November for the following year. See HR-100.

## Public holidays

**Q: How many public holidays does Tulpix recognize in the Netherlands?**
A: 11 in 2026 (including Good Friday, which Tulpix pays even though it's not legally required). See `02-public-holidays.csv`.

**Q: I work in Berlin. Which holidays apply to me?**
A: German federal holidays plus those specific to Berlin (Berlin has fewer than Bavaria). The CSV lists all 5 country sets. The principle: **you follow the public holiday calendar of your work location**.

**Q: What if a public holiday falls on a weekend?**
A: There is **no automatic substitute day** in the Netherlands. Other EU countries vary (e.g., the UK has bank-holiday substitution, but the UK is not in scope here).

## Parental, family, sick leave

**Q: How much paid maternity leave do I get in the Netherlands?**
A: **16 weeks total** (zwangerschaps- en bevallingsverlof), paid at 100% of salary by UWV (capped at the daily maximum). Tulpix tops up any UWV cap so you receive 100% of your actual salary. See HR-200.

**Q: How much paid partner leave do I get?**
A: **1 week fully paid** (geboorteverlof) immediately after birth + up to **5 weeks** at 70% (aanvullend geboorteverlof) within 6 months, paid by UWV. Tulpix tops up to 100% for both. See HR-200.

**Q: How much paid parental leave?**
A: Each parent gets **9 weeks paid parental leave at 70%** (UWV, capped) within the child's first year, plus **17 unpaid weeks** until age 8. Tulpix tops up the paid 9 weeks to **100%**. See HR-200.

**Q: I'm sick. How much sick pay do I get?**
A: Year 1: **100% of salary**. Year 2: **70% of salary** (statutory minimum), with Tulpix topping up the first 6 months of year 2 to 90%. See HR-210.

**Q: Do I have to disclose what's wrong when I'm sick?**
A: **No.** Per UAVG and AP guidance, you may not be asked for a diagnosis. You only share whether you can work and an expected return date. The company doctor handles medical details confidentially. See HR-210 and HR-800.

## Working hours, remote work

**Q: What is the standard working week?**
A: **40 hours** in the Netherlands; varies by country (38 in DE, 35 in FR for cadres exempt). See HR-300.

**Q: Can I work fully remote from another EU country?**
A: Yes, **up to 60 days per year** outside your country of employment without additional approval. Beyond that, social-security and tax rules require a formal review. See HR-310.

**Q: Can I work remotely from outside the EU/EEA?**
A: Only with **prior approval from HR + Legal** and limited to **30 days per year**, and only in countries with a tax treaty with the Netherlands. See HR-310.

**Q: How many days per week must I be in the office?**
A: Default hybrid expectation is **2 days per week** in the office, with team flexibility. Fully-remote roles are designated by the role, not the individual. See HR-310.

**Q: Do I get a home-office allowance?**
A: Yes — **€2 per home-office day** (Dutch tax-free thuiswerkvergoeding) plus a one-time **€750 setup allowance**. See HR-310 and HR-810.

**Q: Right to disconnect — can my manager email me at 22:00?**
A: They can **send** it (people work in different timezones), but you have **no obligation to read or respond** outside working hours. Repeat violations should be flagged to HR. See HR-300.

## Compensation, benefits, pension

**Q: How often is my salary reviewed?**
A: Once per year, in the **annual compensation review (April)**. Off-cycle increases are possible for promotions and significant scope changes. See HR-400.

**Q: How is my bonus calculated?**
A: Annual bonus = **(target % × base salary) × company-performance multiplier × individual-performance multiplier**. Target % depends on level: IC: 5%, Senior IC: 10%, Manager: 15%, Director: 20%, VP+: 25%. See HR-400.

**Q: Am I auto-enrolled in the pension?**
A: Yes. Employees in NL are auto-enrolled in **PFZW** (or sector equivalent). Tulpix contributes **17%** and employees **5%** of pensionable salary. Opt-out within 30 days of joining; not recommended. See HR-410.

**Q: What's the 30% ruling?**
A: A Dutch tax facility for foreign-recruited employees with scarce skills. **Up to 30% of gross salary is tax-free** for up to **5 years** (reduced from 8 years in 2019, and from 30% to 27% from 2027 per current legislation). See HR-420.

**Q: Do I qualify for the 30% ruling?**
A: Generally yes if (1) recruited from abroad, (2) lived >150 km from the NL border for 16 of the last 24 months, and (3) your gross salary exceeds the annual threshold (~€46,107 in 2026, lower for under-30s with a Master's). Final eligibility decided by Belastingdienst. See HR-420.

## Travel & expense

**Q: What's the daily per diem?**
A: NL: €60, DE/FR/IE: €70, ES: €65. Covers meals and incidentals. See HR-600.

**Q: Can I book my own flights?**
A: Yes, via **Egencia** (the company travel tool). Personal bookings are reimbursable only with prior approval from your manager and may not include premium upgrades. See HR-600.

**Q: How long do I have to submit expenses?**
A: Within **30 days** of incurring the expense, with receipts attached in Pleo. Expenses older than 60 days are not reimbursable except in exceptional cases. See HR-600.

## Code of conduct, DEI, grievance

**Q: Can I accept a gift from a vendor?**
A: Up to **€50 per vendor per year**, declared in the gifts register. Anything above must be declined or donated. See HR-500.

**Q: Can I do paid work outside Tulpix?**
A: Yes, with prior written notification, provided it (a) doesn't compete, (b) doesn't conflict, and (c) doesn't impair your performance. See HR-500.

**Q: Where do I report harassment or discrimination?**
A: To your manager, HR, the **vertrouwenspersoon** (confidential counsellor — internal or external), or the EAP. For formal investigations: HR-520.

**Q: Where do I report a serious legal or ethical concern?**
A: Via the **independent whistleblower hotline** (SpeakUp, run by an external provider) or directly to the Audit Committee. Anonymous reporting is supported. Whistleblowers are protected under the **Wet bescherming klokkenluiders** and EU Directive 2019/1937. See HR-520.

## Training & development

**Q: What's my annual learning budget?**
A: **€2,000 per full-time employee per year**, pro-rated for part-time. Does not roll over. See HR-700.

**Q: Can I use my budget for language classes?**
A: Yes — particularly the language of your office country. See HR-700.

**Q: Will Tulpix sponsor a Master's or MBA?**
A: For Master's-level programs Tulpix may co-fund up to **€10,000 per year for 2 years**, with a study-cost repayment clause if you leave within 24 months. Approval by VP and Head of Talent. See HR-700.

## Onboarding

**Q: What's my probation period in the Netherlands?**
A: **1 month** for fixed-term contracts <2 years, **2 months** for ≥2 years or indefinite contracts. See HR-710.

**Q: Who is my buddy?**
A: Assigned by your hiring manager before Day 1. A peer (not in your reporting line) who helps you settle in for the first 90 days. See HR-710.

**Q: When do I get my laptop?**
A: It is delivered to your office or home **3 business days before Day 1**. Default: MacBook Pro M4 for engineering, MacBook Air M4 or ThinkPad X1 for non-engineering. See HR-710.

## Data protection (GDPR)

**Q: Who can see my salary data?**
A: HR, payroll, your direct manager, and (in masked form) your skip-level manager. No one else. See HR-800.

**Q: Can my manager read my Slack DMs?**
A: **No** — not without DPO approval and only as part of a documented investigation. See HR-800.

**Q: How long is my data kept after I leave?**
A: Depends on the data: payroll **7 years** (tax law), performance reviews **2 years**, ID copy **5 years**, application materials **4 weeks** (or 1 year with consent). See HR-800.

**Q: How do I get a copy of my data (DSAR)?**
A: Submit a request via the privacy portal on the intranet. We respond within **1 month** (extendable by 2 for complex requests). See HR-800.

## Health, safety, well-being

**Q: How do I access the Employee Assistance Program?**
A: Directly with **OpenUp** (openup.com), no HR involvement, fully confidential. **8 free sessions per year** with a psychologist, plus crisis line 0800-0113. See HR-810.

**Q: Who pays for my computer glasses?**
A: Tulpix reimburses up to **€150 every 3 years** for prescription screen glasses, with an eye exam every 2 years. See HR-810.

**Q: What's the lactation policy?**
A: A private lactation room at every Tulpix office and **paid lactation breaks up to 25% of working time** for the first 9 months after birth. See HR-810.

## Termination & notice

**Q: What's my notice period?**
A: Default for employees in NL: **1 month** (employee notice). Employer notice scales with tenure (1 month <5 yrs, up to 4 months ≥15 yrs), plus a Tulpix +1 month bonus for indefinite contracts >2 years. See HR-900.

**Q: When does notice start?**
A: From the **end of the calendar month** in which you give notice (NL convention). See HR-900.

**Q: How is severance (transitievergoeding) calculated?**
A: **1/3 of monthly salary per year of service**, capped at €98,000 (2026) or 1 year's salary, whichever is higher. See HR-900.

**Q: Will I get a reference?**
A: Yes — under BW 7:656 you have a right to a written reference (getuigschrift) on request. We provide an evaluation if it is positive or neutral. See HR-900.
