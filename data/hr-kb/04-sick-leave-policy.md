# Sick Leave Policy

**Document ID:** HR-210
**Version:** 3.0
**Effective date:** 1 January 2026
**Owner:** People & Culture
**Applies to:** All Tulpix employees in the EU

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy describes how Tulpix manages employee sick leave, in compliance with the Dutch Wet verbetering poortwachter (Gatekeeper Improvement Act) and the EU OSH Framework Directive (89/391/EEC).

## 2. Reporting absence

### 2.1 Same-day notification

If you are unable to work due to illness, you must:

1. **Notify your direct manager before 09:30** local time on the first day of absence — by phone, Slack, or email.
2. **Log the absence in Workday** the same day under *Time Off > Sick Leave*.
3. Indicate the expected duration if known.

If your manager is unavailable, contact your skip-level manager or people@tulpix.example.

### 2.2 Continued absence

For absences longer than 1 day, contact your manager **at least every 3 working days** with a status update. The Tulpix company doctor (bedrijfsarts, ArboNed Amsterdam) may contact you to assess fitness to work.

### 2.3 Recovery

Notify your manager and update Workday on the day you return to work. No medical certificate is required for absences under 7 calendar days, in line with the Dutch principle of medical confidentiality (Tulpix may not ask for diagnosis details).

## 3. Pay during illness

### 3.1 First 52 weeks

Tulpix pays **100% of your regular gross salary** during the first 52 weeks of illness. This exceeds the statutory minimum (Dutch law requires at least 70%, with at least the statutory minimum wage).

### 3.2 Weeks 53–104

Pay is reduced to **70% of regular gross salary** during weeks 53 through 104, in line with statutory minimums (Art. 7:629 BW). Tulpix may offer voluntary top-ups based on individual circumstances and reintegration progress.

### 3.3 Maximum

Sick pay is capped at the **maximum daily wage** set annually by UWV (in 2026: €283.16/day gross). Above this cap, Tulpix tops up the difference for the first 52 weeks only.

### 3.4 Holiday allowance

Holiday allowance (8%) and pension contributions continue to accrue as normal during sick leave.

## 4. Reintegration (re-integratie)

The Wet verbetering poortwachter sets out a strict process for both employer and employee. Key milestones:

| Week | Action |
|------|--------|
| Week 1 | Employee reports sick. Manager opens a sick file in Workday. |
| Week 6 | Company doctor performs a problem analysis (probleemanalyse). |
| Week 8 | Plan of Action (Plan van Aanpak) drafted jointly by employee and manager. |
| Every 6 weeks | Progress evaluation. |
| Week 42 | Employer notifies UWV of long-term sickness. |
| Week 52 | First-year evaluation (eerstejaarsevaluatie). Track 1 (return to own role) or Track 2 (alternative role inside or outside Tulpix) is set. |
| Week 87 | Pre-WIA application sent to employee. |
| Week 91 | WIA application submitted to UWV. |
| Week 104 | Statutory sick pay obligation ends. WIA assessment determines next steps. |

**Note:** If Tulpix is found to have not made sufficient reintegration efforts (a "loonsanctie"), the company may be required to continue paying salary for an additional 52 weeks.

## 5. Company doctor and confidentiality

- The company doctor is independent and bound by medical confidentiality.
- Tulpix HR and managers receive **only** information about: fitness to work (yes/no/partial), expected duration, accommodations needed, and reintegration recommendations.
- **No diagnosis information is shared with Tulpix without the employee's explicit consent.**
- Employees have the right to a second opinion (deskundigenoordeel) from UWV at any stage. Cost: €100, reimbursed by Tulpix if requested in good faith.

## 6. Working partially while sick

Where the company doctor advises partial work capacity, employees and managers should agree on a graded return-to-work schedule. Examples:

- Reduced hours per day
- Modified duties
- Working from home
- Phased increase over 4–8 weeks

Sick pay during partial work covers the hours not worked at the same rates as section 3.

## 7. Frequent short-term absence

If an employee is absent (any duration) more than **4 times in a rolling 12-month period**, the manager will schedule a "verzuimgesprek" (absence conversation) to discuss patterns and possible underlying causes. This is not disciplinary; the goal is to identify support needs.

## 8. Disability and WIA

After 104 weeks of continuous illness, UWV assesses long-term disability under the WIA (Werk en Inkomen naar Arbeidsvermogen) scheme. Outcomes:

- **IVA** — Fully and permanently disabled (75% of last earned salary).
- **WGA** — Partially disabled, with capacity to work (varies based on residual capacity).
- **No WIA entitlement** — Employee deemed fit. Employment may end and unemployment benefits (WW) may apply.

Tulpix carries WGA-aanvullingsverzekering (supplementary insurance) for all employees, providing top-up income for partial disability.

## 8.1 Reintegration after WIA

Tulpix supports employees returning to work post-WIA, including:
- Workplace accommodations funded via the no-risk polis (UWV).
- Job carving in collaboration with the manager.
- Up to €5,000 per employee per year for assistive equipment, training, or coaching.

## 9. Sick during vacation

If you fall ill during planned vacation:

1. Notify your manager and HR the same day.
2. Provide proof of illness (e.g., medical certificate from the country you are in).
3. Sick days are credited back to your vacation balance.

This applies only when illness is reported promptly. Retroactive claims after vacation ends are not accepted.

## 10. Mental health and well-being

Tulpix recognizes that mental health is health. The same sick leave rules apply for mental health absences. Confidential support is available via:

- **OpenUp** (Tulpix's EAP): 24/7 access to psychologists, free for all employees and household members. https://openup.example/tulpix
- **HR confidential line:** confidential@tulpix.example
- **Company doctor:** independent medical assessment

## 11. Subsidiary specifics

- **Germany:** Lohnfortzahlung 100% for first 6 weeks, then Krankengeld at 70% via Krankenkasse.
- **France:** Indemnités journalières (IJSS) after 3 days waiting period, with employer top-up under collective agreement.
- **Spain:** Incapacidad temporal — 60% of base from day 4, 75% from day 21. Tulpix tops up to 100% for first 12 months.
- **Ireland:** Statutory Sick Pay (SSP) — 7 days at 70% of gross (cap €110/day) in 2026. Tulpix tops up to 100% for first 12 months.

Local addenda are maintained on the intranet.
